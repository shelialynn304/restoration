SEO & Tracking That Makes Sense

I build websites so business owners can see what is working: who visits, which pages get attention, what people click, and whether Google is starting to send more traffic.

Search Visibility

Show Up Where Customers Are Searching

SEO means making it easier for Google to understand your services, locations, and pages. For local businesses, that means service pages, city pages, FAQs, fast mobile design, and clear page titles.

  • Service pages: pages for what you sell.
  • City pages: pages for areas you serve.
  • Fast mobile pages: because slow sites kill leads.
  • Tracking: phone clicks, forms, views, and events.
